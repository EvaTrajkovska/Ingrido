About Ingrido


Welcome to our web application designed to streamline your meal-kit ordering experience! Our platform offers a seamless process for both customers and employees, ensuring a smooth journey from menu selection to delivery.

Customers can easily browse through our diverse menu options, selecting the meal-kit that best suits their preferences. With a simple click, they can explore detailed information about each dish and effortlessly add it to their cart. Our intuitive interface allows for easy customization, enabling users to adjust quantities and proceed to the checkout page with ease.

For those looking to expand our culinary offerings, our employee portal provides a convenient platform for adding new recipes and meals. Staff members can access this feature from the main page, entering all necessary details into a user-friendly form.

Whether you're a customer seeking culinary inspiration or an employee eager to contribute to our menu, our web application is designed to meet your needs efficiently and effectively. Join us today and embark on a culinary adventure with ease!
