Step by step guide


1. Go to our 'Menus' page and browse through our menus.

2. Click on a menu that you like and see the offered meals.

3. Choose a meal and enter the quantity of it. Now your meal is added to your cart.

4. If you are satisfied with your cart items proceed to order.

5. Enter your order information and proceed to payment.

5. Enter your billing information and you are done. Your meal is on its way!
